About Us

Hey there! We’re Yke, Djiego, and Naoufel—three friends who love two things: gaming and getting way too philosophical about it. So naturally, we made a podcast where we dive into the deeper meaning behind the games we play. Why just enjoy a game when you can ask the big questions like, “What does this say about free will?” or “Is my in-game character having an existential crisis?” Join us on a journey where we explore the philosophical side of gaming, laugh at ourselves, and maybe answer life’s most profound questions... or at least figure out why that boss fight felt like a personal attack. Ready to level up your thinking? Hit play and let's go!
